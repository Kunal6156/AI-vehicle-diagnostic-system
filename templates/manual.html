üîß Common Problems & Solutions

Starting Issues

Symptoms: Engine cranks but doesn't start, no crank no start

Possible Causes:

  • Dead battery
  • Faulty starter motor
  • Fuel pump issue
  • Ignition switch problem

AC Not Cooling

Symptoms: Weak cooling, no cooling, strange noise

Possible Causes:

  • Low refrigerant (AC gas)
  • Compressor failure
  • Condenser blockage
  • AC belt issue

Brake Problems

Symptoms: Squeaking noise, soft pedal, vibration

Possible Causes:

  • Worn brake pads
  • Brake fluid leak
  • Air in brake lines
  • Warped brake discs

Engine Overheating

Symptoms: Temperature gauge in red zone, steam from hood

Possible Causes:

  • Coolant leak
  • Thermostat failure
  • Radiator blockage
  • Water pump failure
